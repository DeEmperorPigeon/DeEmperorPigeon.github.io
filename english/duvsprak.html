Introduction

Dúvsprak is a project that has been going on for over a year, with me rebooting it every few months. However, this is meant to be the final iteration, and I will continually work and improve this version indefinitely. It is far from being in a useful state, as I have yet to complete the syntax, morphology, aspect, and mood, but already boasts a good 200+ word lexicon.
